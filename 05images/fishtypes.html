Trout Species

Utah is best know for their trout fishing. There are many different species making it fun for all anglers to explore. There are even more sub species of trout such as different cutthroat trout, but these are the main ones.

  1. Rainbow Trout
  2. Tiger Trout
  3. Brown Trout
  4. Cutthrout Trout
  5. Brook Trout
  6. Lake Trout
  7. Golden Trout
